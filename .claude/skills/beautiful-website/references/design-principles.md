# My Design Principles

## Aesthetic Preferences
- I lean toward designs that feel intentional and crafted, not template-y
- I appreciate dark, moody aesthetics with warm accent colors — but I'm open to light themes if they have character
- Typography should feel like it was chosen by a human, not a default dropdown
- I like texture — grain overlays, subtle gradients, layered elements
- Analog warmth over digital coldness (think vinyl sleeve design, letterpress, editorial print)

## Things I Love
- Dramatic typography as a hero element
- Scroll-triggered animations that feel smooth, not gimmicky
- Asymmetric layouts that guide the eye
- Color palettes that feel unexpected but cohesive
- Hover states that reward curiosity

## Things I Hate
- Generic SaaS landing page look (white bg, blue buttons, card grids)
- Overuse of gradients, especially purple-to-blue
- Animations that feel like they're showing off rather than serving the content
- Sites that look the same as every other AI-generated site
- Tiny gray text on white backgrounds
